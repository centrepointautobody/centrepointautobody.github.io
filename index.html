Dent and Scratch Removal

Don’t leave that dent unrepaired! At Centre Point Auto Body, we offer expert dent and scratch removal services. Dents can lead to structural and cosmetic issues, potentially decreasing your vehicle's value. Our professional team will address any dent, regardless of size, to protect your investment and restore your vehicle’s appearance.

Vehicle Refinishing

Your vehicle's paint does more than just enhance its appearance; it provides essential protection. Our vehicle refinishing services use industry-leading automotive paints and products to ensure a factory-like finish that shields your car from the elements. Trust Centre Point Auto Body to restore your vehicle's paint and protect its value!

Insurance Claims

Dealing with a car collision can be stressful, but Centre Point Auto Body is here to help. We assist you throughout the insurance claims process, working directly with insurance companies on your behalf. Plus, we offer a lifetime warranty on all body and paint repairs, ensuring your peace of mind.

Free Same-Day Estimates

Need a quick estimate for your insurance claim or fender bender? Visit Centre Point Auto Body for FREE same-day estimates! You can also email us photos of the damage, and we will provide a detailed estimate for the necessary repairs promptly.
